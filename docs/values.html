The values behind The Garden

The Garden is built on calm technology and humane design. It is a daily companion for the quiet, consistent love that holds — not a productivity tool, not a social feed.

Being good all the way through matters

Consistent, loving presence gives a child a felt sense of safety they can't yet name. The app's whole purpose is to help you notice and keep that.

The love ledger is evidence

Every small act, logged quietly, is proof you never stopped being their mum. On hard days, read it back.

Healing is part of staying in the fight

Rest isn't retreat. You can't pour from an empty cup. The gentle reminders — to breathe, to eat, to move, to sit in nature — are how you stay standing.

Solidarity is survival

The wisdom in this app comes from mums who've lived it and from established family-separation practice. You're part of something bigger than your case file.

How the app treats you

It never shames you (no streaks to break; the ledger only grows). It never demands your attention (one warm card at a time). It works offline. It's private by default — no accounts, no tracking, no analytics. It's safe to be seen with — a discreet name and a PIN, like a locked diary.

What it is not

It's not legal advice. It's not a replacement for crisis services. It can't fix the other parent or the system. If you're in danger or in crisis, please reach out to the services listed in the app's Help section — they're there 24/7 where noted, and they will believe you.

The Garden is independent and not affiliated with Parents Beyond Breakup or any listed service; links are included because they're the right people to call.
