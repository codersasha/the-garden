The Garden — your privacy

This is a calm, private companion app. Privacy here is a safety feature, not a preference.

What's stored

Everything you log — quiet victories, letters, memories, saved affirmations, your settings, shop inventory, and petals — is stored only on your device (in your browser's local storage). There is no server, no account, and no cloud sync.

What leaves your device

By default, nothing. The app makes zero external network calls when the AI feature is off.

The only feature that can send data off your device is AI-assisted writing (BIFF replies and letter drafting), which is off by default. If you turn it on, the text you paste or write is sent to the AI provider you choose (a keyless default, or your own API key). That relationship is between you and that provider. The app adds no tracking of its own.

What we don't collect

No analytics. No telemetry. No advertising identifiers. No accounts. No cookies beyond what your browser uses to store the app itself.

Your PIN

Your lock PIN is never stored in plain text. Only a salted hash (PBKDF2) is kept, so the raw PIN can't be read from your device.

Export & delete

You can export a full backup at any time (Settings → Export my garden) and move it to your own cloud drive or email. You can wipe everything on a device from Settings → Wipe all data. There is nothing on a server to delete, because there is no server.
