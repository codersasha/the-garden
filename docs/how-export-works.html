How backups work

Because everything lives on your device, losing or breaking your phone means losing your ledger unless you've made a backup. A monthly backup is a good rhythm. The app gives you a gentle, dismissible reminder each month.

To back up

  1. Open the app → Settings → Export my garden.
  2. A file called garden-backup-YYYY-MM-DD.json downloads.
  3. Save it somewhere safe: email it to yourself, or put it in iCloud / Google Drive.

The backup excludes your PIN hash by default, so the file alone can't unlock your diary.

To restore (same phone or a new one)

  1. Install/open The Garden on the new device and set a PIN.
  2. Settings → Restore from backup → pick your saved .json file.
  3. Choose to merge with any existing data, or replace it entirely.

Your letters, memories, ledger, inventory, petals and settings come back exactly as they were.
